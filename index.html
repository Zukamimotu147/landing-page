Welcome to Vintage Vehicles

Vintage vehicles are defined by RA 11698 as motor vehicles that is at least 40 years old from the official date of manufacturing. A vintage vehicle's general appearance must not have been altered, and its chassis, engine, steering assembly, and suspension assembly should be either original or authentic.
